Foreign Language - Swahili

Swahili is a Bantu language spoken in Congo and some part of Africa, I would like to speak swahili beacause of couple of reasons. Besides it being an easy to learn and read. I had a friend from Congo who spoke the language, he kept saying Asante until I eventually came to understan that means thank you in english, in all that cought my attention in wanting to learn the language swahili, it seemed to be a language filled with respect.

Nice to meet you Nimefurahi kukutana nawe
Keep well Weka vizuri
