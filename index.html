Forein Language

Swahili is a Bantu language spoken in Congo and some part of Africa, I would like to speak swahili beacause of couple of reasons besides it being easy to learn and read. I had a friend from Congo who spoke the language, he kept saying Asante now and then, eventually came to understan that it means thank you, that cought my attention in wanting to learn swahili, it seemed to be a language filled with respect.

English Swahili
Good morning Habari za asubuhi
Nice to meet you Nimefurahi kukutana nawe
Keep well Weka vizuri
Is good to be here Ni vizari kuma hapa
Thank you Asante
